REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL - COMMERCIAL PAINTING PROJECT

Project Title: Asian Paints Corporate Office Renovation
Client: ABC Technologies Limited
Date: December 2025
Project ID: AP-2025-001

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
ABC Technologies Limited is seeking proposals for comprehensive interior and exterior painting services for our corporate office building located in Mumbai. The project involves painting approximately 50,000 square feet of interior space and 20,000 square feet of exterior facade.

SCOPE OF WORK:

1. INTERIOR PAINTING (50,000 sq ft):
   - Office spaces: 30,000 sq ft
   - Conference rooms: 8,000 sq ft  
   - Common areas: 7,000 sq ft
   - Cafeteria: 5,000 sq ft

2. EXTERIOR PAINTING (20,000 sq ft):
   - Building facade: 15,000 sq ft
   - Parking structure: 5,000 sq ft

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:

Interior Requirements:
- Premium washable paint for high-traffic areas
- Anti-microbial coating for healthcare-grade facilities
- Low VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) paints
- Color scheme: Corporate blue and white palette
- Finish: Semi-gloss for walls, matte for ceilings

Exterior Requirements:
- Weather-resistant exterior paint system
- UV protection coating
- Anti-fungal treatment
- Color: Light beige with dark brown accents
- Finish: Eggshell finish with protective clear coat

PROJECT TIMELINE:
- Project Start Date: January 15, 2026
- Expected Completion: March 30, 2026
- Working Hours: 9 AM - 6 PM, Monday to Saturday
- Total Duration: 75 days

QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Minimum 10-year warranty on exterior paint
- 5-year warranty on interior paint
- ISO 9001 certified materials
- Trained and certified painting crew
- Daily progress reports
- Site supervisor presence throughout project

BUDGET CONSIDERATIONS:
- Total project budget: ₹8,000,000 - ₹12,000,000
- Payment terms: 30% advance, 40% mid-project, 30% on completion
- Include cost of materials, labor, equipment, and taxes
- Separate pricing for interior and exterior work

EVALUATION CRITERIA:
1. Technical capability and experience (30%)
2. Pricing competitiveness (25%)
3. Project timeline adherence (20%)
4. Quality assurance measures (15%)
5. Safety compliance (10%)

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Must use Asian Paints products exclusively
- Environmentally friendly disposal methods
- Minimal disruption to ongoing operations
- Weekend work available if needed
- Insurance coverage of ₹5,000,000

SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS:
Please provide detailed proposal including:
- Company profile and relevant experience
- Detailed project methodology
- Complete cost breakdown
- Project timeline with milestones
- Quality assurance plan
- Safety procedures and certifications
- References from similar projects
