**REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP) - EXTERIOR & INTERIOR PAINTING SERVICES**

**Project Title:** Repainting of "Prestige Lakeside Habitat" Residential Complex
**RFP Reference No:** PLH-2025-MAINT-001
**Date:** December 11, 2025
**Submission Deadline:** December 25, 2025

**1. Client Information**
   - **Organization:** Prestige Estates Projects Ltd.
   - **Contact Person:** Mr. Rajesh Kumar, Facility Manager
   - **Location:** Whitefield, Bengaluru, Karnataka
   - **Email:** facilities@prestigconstructions.com

**2. Project Scope**
   The project involves the complete repainting of the exterior facades of 5 Towers (Tower A to E) and the interior common areas (Lobbies, Staircases).

   **A. Exterior Painting (Towers A-E)**
   - **Total Surface Area:** Approx. 450,000 Sq. Ft.
   - **Current Condition:** Existing weather-proof paint is ensuring fading and algal growth in patches. Cracks observed on south-facing walls.
   - **Requirement:**
     - Thorough cleaning and pressure washing.
     - Crack filling using high-performance exterior putty.
     - Application of 1 coat of Exterior Wall Primer.
     - Application of 2 coats of **Asian Paints Apex Ultima Protek** (or equivalent high-performance exterior emulsion with anti-algal properties).
     - Warranty Required: Minimum 7 Years.

   **B. Interior Painting (Common Areas)**
   - **Total Surface Area:** Approx. 120,000 Sq. Ft.
   - **Requirement:**
     - Surface preparation (sanding and minor repairs).
     - Application of 1 coat of Interior Primer.
     - Application of 2 coats of **Asian Paints Royale Luxury Emulsion** (Matt Finish) for lobbies.
     - Application of 2 coats of **Asian Paints Apcolite Premium Emulsion** for staircases.
   - **Color:** "Morning Glory" (Lobbies) and "Classic White" (Staircases).

**3. Project Timeline & Delivery**
   - **Start Date:** January 15, 2026
   - **Completion Deadline:** March 31, 2026 (Strict deadline before monsoon).
   - **Working Hours:** 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM only (Residential zone restrictions).

**4. Submission Requirements**
   - detailed BOM (Bill of Materials).
   - Commercial Bid with detailed breakdown of material, labor, and overheads.
   - Project Schedule (Gantt Chart).
   - Safety Plan for working at heights (Scaffolding/Cradle systems).

**5. Terms & Conditions**
   - **Payment Terms:**
     - 20% Advance against Bank Guarantee.
     - 70% Progress Payments based on tower completion.
     - 10% Retention held for 6 months post-completion.
   - **Penalty:** 0.5% of contract value per week of delay.
   - **Safety:** Contractor is fully responsible for worker safety and PPE.

**6. Evaluation Criteria**
   - Product quality and warranty (40%)
   - Price competitiveness (30%)
   - Past experience in high-rise painting (30%)
