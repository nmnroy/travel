**REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP) - EXTERIOR & INTERIOR PAINTING SERVICES**

**Project Title:** Repainting of "Prestige Lakeside Habitat" Residential Complex
**RFP Reference No:** PLH-2025-MAINT-001
**Date:** December 11, 2025
**Location:** Whitefield, Bengaluru, Karnataka

**2. Project Scope**
   The project involves the complete repainting of the exterior facades of 5 Towers (Tower A to E) and the interior common areas.

   **A. Exterior Painting (Towers A-E)**
   - **Total Surface Area:** Approx. 450,000 Sq. Ft.
   - **Requirement:**
     - 1 coat of Exterior Wall Primer.
     - 2 coats of **Asian Paints Apex Ultima Protek** (Performance Warranty Required: 7 Years).

   **B. Interior Painting (Towers A-E Types)**
   - **Total Surface Area:** Approx. 120,000 Sq. Ft.
   - **Requirement:**
     - 2 coats of **Asian Paints Royale Luxury Emulsion** (Matt Finish) for lobbies.
     - 2 coats of **Asian Paints Apcolite Premium Emulsion** for staircases.

**3. Project Timeline**
   - **Completion Deadline:** March 31, 2026 (Strict deadline before monsoon).
   - **Working Hours:** 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM only.

**5. Terms & Conditions**
   - **Payment:** 20% Advance, 70% Progress, 10% Retention.
   - **Penalty:** 0.5% of contract value per week of delay.
